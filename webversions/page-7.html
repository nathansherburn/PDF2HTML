CONCLUSION
A modi ed TLUD stove has been produced, tested
and compared with a traditional three-stone re.
The results indicate that variations in geometry of
the TLUD chimney are essential to ensure improved
combustion. Results also show that when burning
dung, the TLUD with optimised chimney geometry,
is far superior to a three-stone re in regards to
CO emissions, but due to the higher burn rate, can
produce higher levels of NO
X, and CO
2(eq). Results
also indicate that fuel preparation is important to
ensure correct TLUD operation.
